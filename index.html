CLI Chess

Chess, written entirely in the Ruby programming language and playable in the command line. Utilizing OOP principles to keep my code clean and modular, I built a program with all the rules of a real game of Chess: an 8x8 board, pieces with restricted movements, pawn promotion, en passant, castling, check and checkmate. Players can save their games and play locally against another human or a CPU.

About Me

Hey, I'm Jarrod. In 2022, I got the itch to learn a new skill. After a bit of searching I discovered The Odin Project and started building toy apps in JavaScript/Ruby and pushing them to Github. I found it exciting to learn new things every day and build increasingly complex apps. I loved getting into the flow state while solving novel problems, and seeing my projects grow from a single line of code to a complete app. Now, I'm hoping to continue learning at a company.

In 2020, I spent a year in the California Conservation Corps building trails, planting trees and participating in the salmon restoration project.
